About the studio

Where every bride
looks her most radiant self

Shyni Makeover Studio, led by makeup artist Lavanya Anil Reddy, is a Karimnagar-based bridal beauty studio specialising in South Indian, North Indian and traditional bridal looks — built on the belief that makeup should enhance, not mask, who you are.

From the first Haldi to the final Reception twirl, our team handles makeup, hair, draping and mehendi so you can simply enjoy the celebration. Our in-house bridal photo studio means your finished look gets captured the same day, under proper studio lighting.
